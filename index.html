What is Ratcoin?

Fast peer-to-peer transactions

Ratcoin uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority or banks; managing transactions and the issuing of ratcoins is carried out collectively by the network. Ratcoin is open-source; its design is public, nobody owns or controls ratcoin and everyone can take part. Through many of its unique properties, ratcoin allows exciting uses that could not be covered by any previous payment system.
